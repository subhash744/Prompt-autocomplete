Privacy Policy
Prompt Autocomplete Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: We collect zero data. Everything you save stays on your device only. We have no servers, no accounts, no tracking.

1. Who We Are
Prompt Autocomplete is a Chrome browser extension that helps you save, organize, and reuse prompts on AI platforms including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok. The extension is an independent product and is not affiliated with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or xAI.

2. Data We Collect
We collect absolutely no personal data.

The extension does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any information about you or your activity. Specifically, we do not collect:

Your name, email address, or any account information
The prompts you save or type
Your browsing history or activity on AI platforms
Usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports
IP addresses or device identifiers
Any form of personally identifiable information
3. Local Storage Only
All data created by the extension — including your saved prompts, tags, site assignments, pin states, and usage counts — is stored exclusively in Chrome's local extension storage on your own device using the standard chrome.storage.local API.

This data:

Never leaves your device
Is never sent to any server
Is never accessible to us or any third party
Is deleted when you uninstall the extension
Can be cleared at any time from Chrome's extension settings
4. Permissions We Request and Why
The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

storage — Required to save your prompts locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data leaves your device.
Host access to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok — Required to inject the autocomplete UI into these AI chat pages so suggestions appear as you type. The extension reads and writes only to the prompt input field and does not access any other content on these pages.
We request the minimum permissions necessary to provide core functionality. We do not request permissions for browsing history, bookmarks, cookies, identity, or any other sensitive browser data.

5. No Third-Party Services
The extension does not integrate with, load from, or communicate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs. There are no external scripts, fonts, or resources loaded by the extension. All code runs entirely within your browser.

6. No Account Required
The extension works without creating an account or providing any personal information. There is no registration, login, or sign-up process.

7. Children's Privacy
The extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we collect no data whatsoever, the extension is safe for all ages from a data privacy standpoint.

8. Changes to This Policy
If we ever change our data practices — for example, if a future version introduces optional cloud sync — this privacy policy will be updated clearly before such a version is released. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

9. Open Source Transparency
The full source code of this extension is available for review. We encourage technically inclined users to inspect the code directly to verify our privacy claims. The extension contains no obfuscated code.

10. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data handling, you can reach us via the Chrome Web Store support tab on the extension's listing page.
