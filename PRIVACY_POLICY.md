Privacy Policy
Prompt Autocomplete Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: We collect zero data. Everything you save stays on your device only. No servers, no accounts, no tracking.

1. Who We Are
Prompt Autocomplete is a Chrome browser extension that helps you save, organize, and reuse prompts on AI platforms including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok. The extension is an independent product and is not affiliated with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or xAI.

2. Data We Collect
We collect absolutely no personal data. The extension does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any information about you or your activity. Specifically, we do not collect:

Your name, email address, or any account information
The prompts you save or type
Your browsing history or activity on AI platforms
Usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports
IP addresses or device identifiers
Any form of personally identifiable information
3. Local Storage Only
All data created by the extension — including your saved prompts, tags, site assignments, pin states, and usage counts — is stored exclusively in Chrome's local extension storage on your own device using the standard chrome.storage.local API.

Never leaves your device
Never sent to any server
Never accessible to us or any third party
Deleted when you uninstall the extension
4. Permissions We Request and Why
storage — Saves your prompts locally on your device. No data leaves your device.
Host access to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok — Injects the autocomplete UI into prompt input fields only. No other page content is accessed.
5. No Third-Party Services
The extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external APIs. All code runs entirely within your browser.

6. No Account Required
The extension works without creating an account or providing any personal information.

7. Children's Privacy
The extension collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy
If we ever change our data practices, this policy will be updated before such changes take effect.

9. Contact
Questions? Reach us via the Chrome Web Store support tab on the extension listing page.
