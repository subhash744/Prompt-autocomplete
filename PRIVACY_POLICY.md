Privacy Policy — Prompt Autocomplete Chrome Extension
Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: We collect zero data. Everything you save stays on your device only. No servers, no accounts, no tracking.


1. Who We Are
Prompt Autocomplete is a Chrome browser extension that helps you save, organise, and reuse prompts across AI tools including ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Grok, DeepSeek, Perplexity, Meta AI, Midjourney, Runway, HeyGen, and 30+ other AI chatbots, image generators, and video generators.
The extension is an independent product built by a solo developer. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Microsoft, xAI, Meta, Midjourney, Runway, or any other AI company.

2. Data We Collect
We collect absolutely no personal data. The extension does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any information about you or your activity. Specifically, we do not collect:

Your name, email address, or any account information
The prompts you save or type
Your browsing history or activity on any AI platform
Usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports
IP addresses or device identifiers
Any form of personally identifiable information


3. Local Storage Only
All data created by the extension — including your saved prompts, tags, site assignments, pin states, and usage counts — is stored exclusively in Chrome's local extension storage on your own device using the standard chrome.storage.local API.

Never leaves your device
Never sent to any server
Never accessible to us or any third party
Automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension

5. Uninstall Feedback (Optional)
When you uninstall the extension, a feedback form hosted by Tally.so may open in a new browser tab. This is completely optional.

If you submit the form, your response is governed by Tally's privacy policy
We use this feedback only to understand why users leave and improve the extension
No data from the extension itself — your prompts, usage counts, or any local data — is included or sent


6. No Third-Party Services Inside the Extension
The extension itself does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external APIs. All extension code runs entirely within your browser.
The only external URLs the extension ever opens are:

Chrome Web Store — when you click "Rate it"
Tally.so — when you click "Give feedback" or uninstall

Both happen only when you explicitly click a button. Nothing is opened automatically.

7. No Account Required
The extension works without creating an account or providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy
The extension collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy
If we ever change our data practices, this policy will be updated before such changes take effect. The Last updated date at the top will reflect any changes.

10. Contact
Questions? Reach us via the Chrome Web Store support tab on the extension listing page.
