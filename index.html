FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before installing.

Which AI platforms does it work on?
ChatGPT (chat.openai.com and chatgpt.com), Claude (claude.ai), Gemini (gemini.google.com), and Grok (grok.com). Works on all 4 with the same saved prompts.
Is my data safe? Does it collect anything?
Your data never leaves your device. Everything is stored locally using Chrome's storage API. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking of any kind.
Is it really free?
Yes. Save up to 25 prompts completely free. No credit card, no account, no subscription. Just install and use.
How do I trigger autocomplete?
Just start typing in any AI chat box — suggestions appear automatically. Press Ctrl+Space to browse all prompts. Type ;tagname to filter by tag.
Can I use different prompts on different AI tools?
Yes. When saving a prompt, select which platforms it should appear on — or leave it as "All Sites" to show everywhere.
What happens to my prompts if I uninstall?
They are deleted along with the extension's local storage. We recommend exporting your prompts as JSON before uninstalling.
